How Screen Flow Pro Works

Screen Flow Pro uses Apple’s ReplayKit broadcast recording system to record your screen across apps.

Recording Steps

  1. Open Screen Flow Pro
  2. Tap Start Broadcast Recording
  3. Confirm the system broadcast prompt
  4. Swipe up and use any app — recording continues in the background
  5. Stop recording from the red indicator or Control Center
  6. Your video is saved automatically to the Photos app

Tip You do not need to keep Screen Flow Pro open while recording.

Where Are My Videos?

All recordings are saved to the Photos app on your device. This behavior is required by iOS for cross-app screen recording.

Free Usage, Credits, and Pro Subscription

  • Free Plan: Includes a limited number of recordings per month.
  • Recording Credit Packs: Optional consumable purchases. Each credit allows one additional recording session once free usage is exhausted.
  • Pro Subscription: Removes all recording limits for unlimited screen recordings.

Restoring Purchases

  • Subscriptions can be restored using the app’s Restore Purchases option.
  • Consumable recording credits cannot be restored once used (this is an App Store rule).

Troubleshooting

Recording doesn’t start

ReplayKit broadcast recording only works on physical iPhone or iPad devices. Screen recording is not supported in the iOS Simulator or on macOS.

Broadcast option doesn’t appear

  1. Open Control Center
  2. Long-press Screen Recording
  3. Ensure Screen Flow Pro appears as a broadcast option

Privacy

Screen Flow Pro does not collect or transmit personal data. All recordings remain on your device. Read the full policy here: Privacy Policy.
