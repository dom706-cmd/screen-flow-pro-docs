Privacy Policy — Screen Flow Pro

Effective Date: January 2026

Screen Flow Pro (“Screen Flow Pro,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Screen Flow Pro mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Do NOT Collect

Screen Flow Pro does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • User accounts or identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage behavior or activity logs

Screen Recordings

Screen Flow Pro allows users to record their device screen using Apple’s ReplayKit broadcast recording system.

  • All screen recordings are created and stored locally on the user’s device
  • Recordings are saved to the Photos app, as required by iOS
  • Screen Flow Pro does not upload, transmit, or share recordings
  • Screen Flow Pro does not analyze or inspect recorded content
  • Screen Flow Pro does not have access to the contents of your Photos library

The user has full control over their recordings and may delete them at any time using the Photos app.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Screen Flow Pro offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions processed entirely by Apple via the App Store.

  • Payment information is handled by Apple
  • Screen Flow Pro does not receive or store payment details
  • Purchase status is verified using Apple’s StoreKit APIs

Data Storage

Any app-related settings (such as subscription status, recording credits, or usage limits) are stored locally on the device using Apple-provided system storage. No data is stored on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Screen Flow Pro uses only Apple-provided frameworks and services, including ReplayKit, StoreKit, and Photos. No third-party SDKs or analytics services are used.

Children’s Privacy

Screen Flow Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected, no special handling is required.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
